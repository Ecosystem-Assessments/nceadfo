9 eDrivers

Along with a description of the distribution and intensity of environmental drivers in the St.Â Lawrence System, Beauchesne et al. (2020) launched an open knowledge platform called eDrivers. This project seeked to shared knowledge on environmental drivers as well as to gather a community of experts whose goal is to structure, standardize and share knowledge on drivers in support of science and maangement. This initiative also sought to uphold the highest standards of data management and open science and was built on 4 guiding principles:

  1. Unity and Inclusiveness
  2. Findability, Accessibility, Interoperability, and Reusability
  3. Adaptiveness
  4. Recognition

As part of this assessment, we updated eDrivers to include the environmental drivers that were identified on the Scotian Shelf. For more details on eDrivers, you may visit the web application and refer to Beauchesne et al. (2020).
